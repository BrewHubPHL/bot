Effective Date: [Date] Between: BrewHub PHL ("The Company") and [Employee Name] ("The Employee")
Article 1: Recognition & Purpose
1.1 Scope: This agreement covers all hourly staff (Baristas, Shift Leads, and Hub Operators) employed at BrewHub PHL, located in Point Breeze, Philadelphia. 1.2 Purpose: The purpose of this agreement is to establish clear, fair, and transparent working conditions, wage rates, and operational protocols that benefit both the business and the neighborhood it serves.
Article 2: Hours of Work, Scheduling, & Time Tracking
2.1 The Workweek: The standard workweek begins on Monday at 12:00 AM and ends on Sunday at 11:59 PM. 2.2 Time Tracking: All time is tracked strictly via the BrewHub Staff Portal (/staff-hub.html). Employees must use their secure PIN to clock in at the start of their shift and out at the end. 2.3 Missed Punches: If an Employee forgets to clock out, the system will flag a "Missing Clock-Out" after 16 hours. The Employee must notify the Manager within 24 hours to manually correct the time log. Habitual missed punches may result in disciplinary action. 2.4 Schedule Changes: Management will post schedules at least [Number, e.g., 14] days in advance. Shift swaps between employees are permitted but must be approved by a Manager via the BrewHub platform.
Article 3: Wages & Premium Pay (Stacking)
3.1 Base Rate: The Employee's starting base wage is $[Amount]/hr. 3.2 Overtime (1.5x): Any hours worked in excess of 40.0 hours within the standard Monday–Sunday workweek will be paid at one-and-one-half (1.5x) the base rate. 3.3 Sunday Premium (1.5x): All regular hours worked on Sundays are paid at a premium rate of 1.5x the base rate. 3.4 Sunday Overtime (2.0x): If an employee has already exceeded 40 hours for the week, any subsequent hours worked on a Sunday will be paid at double time (2.0x) the base rate. 3.5 Pay Periods: Wages are calculated bi-weekly and paid via direct deposit.
Article 4: Health, Safety, & Environment
4.1 Zero Tolerance: BrewHub strictly prohibits harassment, discrimination, or abusive behavior from staff, management, or customers. 4.2 The "Philly Way" Customer Protocol: Staff are empowered to refuse service to any customer who is verbally abusive, threatening, or compromises the safety of the cafe. 4.3 Emergency Protocols: Employees are trained on the use of the silent panic alarm and emergency cash drop procedures. No employee will be held liable for cash drawer shortages resulting from a verified security incident.
Article 5: Discipline & The "Just Cause" Standard
5.1 Just Cause: Unlike standard "at-will" agreements, BrewHub agrees to discipline or discharge employees only for "just cause." This means management must have a valid, documented reason for termination. 5.2 Progressive Discipline: Except in cases of severe misconduct (e.g., theft, violence, severe safety violations), discipline will be progressive:
Step 1: Verbal Warning
Step 2: Written Warning
Step 3: Final Warning / Suspension
Step 4: Termination 5.3 Digital Revocation: Upon termination, the Employee’s digital token version will be incremented in the BrewHub database, instantly revoking access to the POS, KDS, and Parcel Hub.
Article 6: Grievance Procedure
6.1 Dispute Resolution: If the Employee feels this agreement has been violated (e.g., a missing paycheck, unfair discipline), they should first bring it up with the Shift Lead. 6.2 Owner Review: If unresolved, the grievance may be submitted in writing to the Owner. A meeting will be held within 5 business days to discuss and resolve the issue.

Article 8: Entire Agreement & Addenda 
8.1 Incorporation: This Agreement, along with the Profit Sharing Addendum (Exhibit A) attached hereto, constitutes the entire agreement between the parties.
8.2 Acknowledgment: By signing this Agreement, the Employee acknowledges they have received, read, and accepted the terms of the Profit Sharing Addendum, which is incorporated herein by reference. In the event of any conflict between the Main Agreement and the Addendum regarding profit sharing, the terms of the Addendum shall prevail.

📜 Profit Sharing Addendum: BrewHub PHL
1. Eligibility & Vesting
Vesting Period: Participation in the BrewHub Profit Sharing Pool begins after six (6) months of continuous employment.
Probationary Clause: The first 90 days of employment are a training period and do not count toward profit-sharing eligibility.
2. The Profit Sharing Pool Calculation
Profit Floor: The "Sharable Pool" is only generated if the monthly Net Profit (as defined by the BrewHub Manager Dashboard) exceeds a baseline Profit Floor of $5,000.00.
Sharing Percentage: BrewHub PHL will contribute 10% of all Net Profit above the $5,000.00 floor into a quarterly pool for eligible staff.
Net Profit Definition: Revenue minus all Operating Expenses (OpEx), including rent, payroll, Cost of Goods Sold (COGS), and Equipment Maintenance Costs (as tracked in the Asset Health tab).
3. Distribution Logic
Hours-Based Allocation: The pool is distributed based on the total hours worked by each eligible employee during that quarter.
Formula: (Employee Hours / Total Eligible Team Hours) x Total Pool Amount.
4. Transparency & Payout
Dashboard Access: Staff can view the current month's "Profit Share Progress Bar" on the Manager-approved transparency view of the BrewHub Dashboard.
Frequency: Payouts will occur on the first payroll cycle following the end of each fiscal quarter.
